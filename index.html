Shandur polo festival is one of the big festivals held from 7 to 9 July every year in Pakistan. Shandur pass has a beautiful polo ground where a unique free-style polo is played between different teams of the region. This polo ground is considered to be the highest polo ground in the world at 3,700meter. Festival includes folk music, folk dance, traditional sports and a camping village is set up on the pass for the tourists.The festival provides an amazing cultural experience.
